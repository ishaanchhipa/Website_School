Infrastructure

Facilities Offered :

  • Spacious, fully air-conditioned, well-ventilated & well-lit classrooms. All rooms are equipped with Smart Boards supported by the latest technology. This e-teaching, using the software specially developed to suit the CBSE curriculum, makes teaching effective & learner friendly.
  • We have an aesthetically planned huge air-conditioned multi-purpose auditorium for recreational and educational activities. It has a seating capacity of 2000 people and is equipped with state-of-the-art sound and light effects.
  • A mini open-air amphitheater with a seating capacity of 300 students.
  • The foreign language labs provide an ambient atmosphere for the enthusiastic learners of the foreign languages taught in the school. Sanskrit, Hindi and English Labs ensure a strong foundation for the respective languages.
  • A resource center for teachers has also been set up to help teachers update their skills.
  • Specialty Rooms like dance rooms, music rooms, art rooms & audio-visual rooms.
  • Modern & well - equipped Science labs, EVS labs, English language lab.
  • 5 Computer labs with over 220 computers.
  • A Multi-Media lab with latest facilities.
  • The school cafeteria serving healthy & balanced meals.
  • Air-conditioned libraries providing access to over 20,000 books.
  • Two well- equipped clinics with full-time doctors & trained nurses.
  • The Atrium serving as a multi-utility area.
  • Yoga-rooms, gym, swimming-pool, splash-pool.
  • Sports- field, Conference Room.
